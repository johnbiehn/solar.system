The Solar System is a group of planets and moons that orbit our Sun. Here is some information about our Solar System.

There are 8 planets. Those include:

  1. Mercury

  2. Venus

  3. Earth

  4. Mars

  5. Jupiter

  6. Saturn

  7. Uranus

  8. Neptune

Here are the terrestial planets

Terrestrial planets have a rocky surface and are the smaller planets

  • Mercury


  • Venus


  • Earth


  • Mars


Here are the gas planets

Gas planets don't have a solid surface. They are mostly made of hydrogen and helium. They are the larger planets

  • Jupiter


  • Saturn


  • Uranus


  • Neptune
